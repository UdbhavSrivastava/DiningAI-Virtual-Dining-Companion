Saganaki:

Description: Flaming Greek cheese served with lemon wedges and crusty bread.
Ingredients: Kefalotyri or graviera cheese, flour, olive oil, lemon wedges, crusty bread.
Serving Size: One portion, served as a hot appetizer.
Price: $12.99
Recommended Pairings: Enjoy with a glass of ouzo or tsipouro as a traditional Greek appetizer.
